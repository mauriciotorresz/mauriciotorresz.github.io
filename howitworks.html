What is Moober?

Moober makes it fast and easy for landlords to sell or rent property

It allows people to earn money by finding property for others

How It Works

Earn money by finding a property for a friend

  1. Search for properties that may interest people you know
  2. Indicate the name of the people who are interested
  3. Once the deal closes collect your commission

List your property and let us rapidly find you the perfect buyer

  1. List your property
  2. Indicate commission % for brokers
  3. Execute the sale/rent process with our help
